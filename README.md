📊**Ferns & Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard** 

🔹**Overview**    

This project analyzes sales data of Ferns & Petals (FNP) to understand sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance using Microsoft Excel.

🔹**Key Business Questions Answered**
1. **Total Revenue** – Identified the overall revenue generated.
2. **Average Order and Delivery Time** – Evaluated delivery performance.
3. **Monthly Sales Performance** – Analyzed monthly sales trends for 2023.
4. **Top Products by Revenue** – Identified highest revenue-generating products.
5. **Customer Spending Analysis** – Calculated average customer spending.
6. **Sales Performance of Top 5 Products** – Compared top-performing products.
7. **Top 10 Cities by Orders** – Identified cities with highest order volume.
8. **Revenue by Occasion** – Compared revenue across different occasions.
9. **Product Popularity by Occasion** – Analyzed product demand by occasion.

🔹**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
- Total Orders: 1000
- Total Revenue: ₹ 3,520,984
- Average Order Delivery Time: 5.53 days
- Average Customer Spend: ₹ 3520.98

🔹**Key Insights**
- Anniversary & Raksha Bandhan generate highest revenue
- Sales peak in February & August
- Top products contribute major revenue
- Some cities have higher order volume
